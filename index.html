I developed the DiaFit app for iOS plateform at UFHealth. DiaFit helps clients to track their activity and nutrition levels just by the flick of a finger. :)


VO2max Calculator

I designed the VO2max Calculator website for University of Florida Health Institues. This site is the measure of aerobic fitness, one of the most important predictors of future health. It allows users to input their info, do 45 squats and measure their cardiorespiratory fitness level base on heart rate.

NODE

I designed and developed the New Onset Diabetes Educator (NODE) for kids with diabetes. It works like an online book that contains all sorts of information about diabetes using pictures, video, and interactive fun animations I have made, so kids will have better chance to build confidence and fight with their illness! :)
